About Us

Welcome to Sahyadri Camp

Welcome to Sahyadri Camp near Lonavala, your perfect destination for a peaceful yet adventurous lakeside escape. We offer a variety of stays including Tent Camping, Standard Triangle tents, Cozy Cottages, and Cabin Cottages, ensuring the best comfort and unique experiences for every traveler.

Nestled amidst the Sahyadri hills, our campsite is ideal for families, couples, and groups searching for the best camping at Pawna Lake with breathtaking lake views and serene surroundings.
